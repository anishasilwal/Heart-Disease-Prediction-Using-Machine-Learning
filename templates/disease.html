Heart Disease

Heart disease encompasses various conditions affecting the heart and blood vessels, leading to a major cause of global mortality.

Symptoms

  • Chest pain (angina)
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue
  • Irregular heartbeats
  • Dizziness
  • Fainting

Prevention

  • Maintain a healthy diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Quit smoking
  • Limit alcohol consumption
  • Manage stress
  • Control blood pressure and diabetes
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Attend regular check-ups
  • Follow prescribed medications when necessary
